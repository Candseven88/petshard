# Product Overview

PetShard is an AI-powered pet health and care platform that provides comprehensive tools for pet owners. The platform focuses on delivering instant, intelligent pet care solutions through various AI-driven features.

## Core Features

- **Pet Name Generator**: Upload pet photos to get personalized name suggestions with complete personality profiles
- **Pet Health Center**: Symptom checker and breed-specific health guidance
- **Pet Care Guide**: Personalized advice for feeding, training, grooming, and environment setup
- **Breed Identification**: AI-powered breed recognition from photos
- **Virtual Breeding**: Predict offspring appearance
- **Pet Video Generator**: Create fun pet videos
- **Pet Diary**: Record daily pet activities
- **Pet Horoscope**: Zodiac-based pet personality insights
- **Pet Age Calculator**: Convert pet age to human years

## Target Audience

Pet owners seeking professional health advice, care guidance, and AI-powered tools for better pet care. The platform serves both new and experienced pet owners with dogs, cats, and other pets.

## AI Integration

The platform uses Zhipu AI's GLM-4V-Flash model for vision-based analysis and GLM-4-Flash for text generation, providing intelligent responses for health consultations, breed identification, and personalized recommendations.
